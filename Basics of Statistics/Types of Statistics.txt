Types of Statistics:
	1. Descriptive
		- Definition: consist of methods for:
			. Organizing data
			. Displaying data
			. Describing data
		- Tools for applying data:
			. Tables
				. frequency distribution tables
			. Graphs
				. histrograms
				. barplots
			. Summary Measures:
				. Mesures of Central Tendency
					. Mean
					. Mode
					. Median
				. Measures of Dispersion
					. Range
					. Standard Deviation
	2. Inferential
		- Definition: consist of methods that use sample results to make decisions or predictions about a population
		- Tools for applying data:
			. ANOVA
			. Linear regression
			. Decision Trees
			. Neural Networks
