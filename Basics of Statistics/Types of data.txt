Different Types of Data:
	1. Qualitative/Categorial Data
		. Definition: Data tha can classified into two or more NONNUMERIC catagories
		. Two Types:
			- Nominal Data
				. Definition: NON-ORDERED (no RANKING) data
			- Ordinal Data
				. Definition: ORDERED or RANKED Data
	2. Quantitative data
		. Definition: Data that CAN BE measured NUMERICALLY
		. Two Types:
			- Discrete Data
				. Definition: Countable data (finite or countably infinite data)
			- Continuous Data
				. Definition: Uncountable data
